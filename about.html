My Product Philosophy

Ship to Learn

I believe in getting product in front of users fast. At NextGen, we went from concept to 5-community pilot in months, not years. At Oracle, I used POC-driven sales to achieve 90% conversion. The market teaches you things no strategy deck can.

Architecture is a Product Decision

The best PMs understand technical trade-offs at a deep level. Whether it's choosing event-driven over batch processing (Citi: 10hrs→1hr) or on-premise ML over cloud APIs (EVA: HIPAA compliance), architecture decisions ARE product decisions.

Measure What Matters

Revenue, adoption, customer outcomes. Not feature velocity. Every initiative I lead ties back to a business metric: $65M revenue at Microsoft, 30% faster response at NextGen, $200M savings at Citi.

What I'm Looking For

I'm seeking AI PM, Agentic PM, or AI Platform PM roles where I can apply my experience building and scaling AI/ML platforms at companies pushing the frontier of what's possible with AI.
